# Vancouver data license

The pinned specimen snapshot is from the City of Vancouver Public trees inventory and the separate food-site snapshot is from its Community gardens and food trees dataset. Both are subject to the [Open Government Licence - Vancouver](https://opendata.vancouver.ca/pages/licence/). The tree snapshot is a 100-record engineering sample; the food-site snapshot is 170 site-level records, not individual tree or access-permission data. The engine code is MIT licensed; city data and media retain their own licences.
